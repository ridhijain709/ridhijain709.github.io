How it works

The WhatsApp automation flow

For business owners who want to understand the system before buying it.

1

Customer messages on WhatsApp

A patient asks about acne treatment. A distributor asks about MOQ. A student asks about course fees. It arrives like any normal WhatsApp message.

2

System checks your FAQ bank first

We pre-load 15–30 of your most common questions and answers. If the message matches, it answers instantly from your FAQ — no AI cost, no delay.

3

For new questions, AI generates a response

Using your business context (what you sell, your pricing, your rules), the AI writes a reply in your tone. It never invents information it doesn't have.

4

Response sent with a call-to-action

Every reply ends with the next step — book an appointment, fill the enquiry form, get a pricing sheet. The conversation moves forward automatically.

5

Complex cases escalate to you

Complaints, medical questions, large orders, payment issues — the system flags these and alerts you. You only spend time on conversations that actually need you.
